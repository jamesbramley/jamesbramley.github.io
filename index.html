Personal Projects

  • 2D Platformer

    DOWNLOAD

    During my 4th year at university I took a game development submodule. In this submodule I was tasked with creating a game in which you collected 10 'treasures' around the Durham University campus. I created a 2D platformer entitled 'Durham Quest V: Final Treasure Prologue'. The game uses a traditional platformer design combined with a hint of Metroidvania.

    The game was developed by me over the course of 2 months, is completely finished and features 4 levels. My aim with this game was to keep the scale low but have a polished final product.

    The character design and UI elements were created by me, however other assets were taken from pre-existing sources to save time.
