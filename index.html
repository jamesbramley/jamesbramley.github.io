Game Development Portfolio

I am a 4th Year student at Durham University studying Computer Science. I have 2 and a half years of experience using the Unity Game Engine and C# to create both personal and university projects. This website is a portfolio of some of these projects. I have recently picked up Unreal Engine 4 but am yet to develop any projects for it.

Personal Projects

  • 2D Platformer

    DOWNLOAD

    During my 4th year at university I took a game development submodule. In this submodule I was tasked with creating a game in which you collected 10 'treasures' around the Durham University campus. I created a 2D platformer entitled 'Durham Quest V: Final Treasure Prologue'. The game uses a traditional platformer design combined with a hint of Metroidvania.

    The game was developed by me over the course of 2 months, is completely finished and features 4 levels. My aim with this game was to keep the scale low but have a polished final product.

    The character design and UI elements were created by me, however other assets were taken from pre-existing sources to save time.

  • Card Game

    I came up with the idea to create a card game based around the Gaming Society my friends and I attend at University. Initially I came up with the system for the game, then my friends and I created over 200 cards with different effects and stats based on the in-jokes of our Gaming society.

    After the cards were created, I made a virtual playing mat for the cards in which 2 players could build decks and face off against each other. At the moment the functionality is that of a real life playing mat with a few additional features, however when I have time in the future I intend to extend it into a fully polished digital card game.
